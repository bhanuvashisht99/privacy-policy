ProBuddy Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2025

Introduction

ProBuddy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by ProBuddy. This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile application, and its associated services (collectively, our "Service"). By accessing or using our Service, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information from you when you voluntarily provide it to us and when you use our Service.

Information you provide us directly:

  • Account information (e.g., name, email address, phone number, password)
  • Profile information (e.g., profile photo, skills, experience, qualifications)
  • Service listings and requests
  • Communications with other users and ProBuddy
  • Payment information
  • Reviews and ratings

Information we collect automatically:

  • Device information (e.g., IP address, browser type, device type)
  • Usage data (e.g., pages visited, time spent on pages)
  • Location information (with your permission)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Match service providers with clients
  • Send technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Develop new products and services
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With other users as necessary for the operation of the Service
  • With vendors and service providers who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf
  • In response to a request for information if we believe disclosure is in accordance with any applicable law, regulation, or legal process
  • If we believe your actions are inconsistent with our user agreements or policies, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of ProBuddy or others
  • In connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business by another company
  • With your consent or at your direction

Data Security

We use reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no Internet or email transmission is ever fully secure or error-free.

Your Choices

You can update your account information and preferences at any time by logging into your account settings. You can also opt-out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in the emails we send.

Your Data Deletion Rights

At ProBuddy, we respect your right to control your personal data. If you wish to have your account and associated data deleted from our systems, you can submit a request through our dedicated data deletion form:

Data Deletion Request Form

Alternatively, you can email your deletion request to privacy@getprobuddy.com.

When submitting a deletion request, please:

  1. Use the email address associated with your ProBuddy account
  2. Include your full name as registered with ProBuddy
  3. Specify that you are requesting complete account deletion

Upon receiving your request, we will:

  1. Verify your identity to protect your privacy
  2. Process your deletion request within 30 days
  3. Send confirmation when the deletion is complete

Please note that some information may be retained for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes as permitted by law. Any retained data will be handled in accordance with this privacy policy.

For any questions about the data deletion process, please contact privacy@getprobuddy.com.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the Service or by email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@getprobuddy.com.
